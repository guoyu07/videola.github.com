  • Create Netflix-style subscription access to your archive of full-length movies.
  • Create a Hulu-style, ad-supported video archive website.
  • Create a Lynda.com-style video tutorial and training materials subscription website.
  • Create an Amazon.com-style pay-per-view streaming video rental website.
