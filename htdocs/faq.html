Frequently Asked Questions

Who makes Videola?
Videola was originally built by Lullabot for the Drupalize.me website. However, from the very beginning it was intended to be a flexible, general use, video platform.
What is the technology behind Videola?

Videola is a custom distribution of Drupal, a PHP-based open source content management system.

How are you transcoding and delivering the video content?

Our video partner is Ooyala

How much does Videola cost?

Videola is Open Source software. If you are handy with Drupal and the LAMP stack, you can download the Videola distribution and install it for yourself.

However, if you would prefer a proven, hosted solution with support, we can get you up and running with your own customized solution in a matter of days. Rates vary based on customer needs, but the minimal cost for most sites will be around $10,000 for setup and $1,500/mo for hosting, video bandwidth, maintenance, and support.

Contact us for a custom quote and more information.

Can site visitors upload videos to a Videola site?

No. Right now Videola is organized with a few-to-many publishing model. So a few trusted site administrators upload and publish the video content. The platform may support a many-to-many publishing model at some time in the future.
